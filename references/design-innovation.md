# Design Innovation Reference — HaibaoChuangyi / 海洋创意

Based on principles from haibaochuangyi-style design analysis (WeChat article, June 2026).

## Core Principles Applicable to PPT Design

### 1. Visual Storytelling
- Every slide should advance a narrative, not just present data
- Use visual metaphors (charts as landscapes, timelines as journeys)
- Opening slide sets the emotional tone, closing slide delivers the call to action

### 2. Emotional Design
- Color temperature is narrative: warm tones for human stories, cool for data-driven sections
- Typography choices convey personality: rounded sans-serif = approachable, geometric = precise
- Whitespace is emotional breathing room — don't fill every pixel

### 3. Cultural Integration
- Chinese design aesthetics (留白, 意境, 虚实对比) can make corporate decks distinctive
- Avoid Western-centric templates — adapt layout rhythm to Chinese reading patterns
- Localize icons and symbols for the target audience's cultural context

### 4. Contrast Rhythm
- Not just color contrast — variety in slide density creates pacing
- Mix text-heavy slides with visual-heavy slides for audience attention
- Intentional asymmetry (打破对称) signals sophistication
